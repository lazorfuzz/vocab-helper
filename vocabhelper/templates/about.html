About

Vocabulary Helper is a simple tool designed to bulk-define lists of words or phrases. Its uses are manyfold; studying for vocab quizzes and tests comes to mind. It's also a great tool for preparing for the SAT, ACT, and more. You can define a single word as well as any list coming from any format.

If you have any ideas about how to make our tool better, please contact us.

Vocabulary Helper is the first in a line of helper tools. Stay tuned for more tools that help students like you succeed in school!
